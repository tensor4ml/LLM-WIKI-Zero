# Common Safety and Preservation Rules

1. Never modify files under `10_Raw/`.
2. Treat raw sources as immutable.
3. Do not delete Wiki pages without explicit approval.
4. Do not merge, rename, or move pages without explicit approval.
5. Do not perform broad refactors without first reporting the plan.
6. Preserve existing claims and record contradictions.
7. Do not invent unsupported claims.
8. Mark uncertainty explicitly.
9. Do not silently expand scope.
10. Do not use external web research unless explicitly requested or required for current verification.
